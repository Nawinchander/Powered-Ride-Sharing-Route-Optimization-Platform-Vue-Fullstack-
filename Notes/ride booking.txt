Interview Questions
1. Why use Service Layer?

Answer: It separates business logic from controllers, making the code cleaner and easier to maintain.

2. Why use a Store (Pinia)?

Answer: Pinia provides centralized state management so multiple components can share the same ride data.

3. Why use Axios?

Answer: Axios simplifies making HTTP requests to the backend and handling responses.

4. Why use Express Router?

Answer: Routers organize related endpoints into separate modules, improving project structure.
